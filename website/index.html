Research Summary

This project demonstrates a unified hybrid quantum-classical AI pipeline. On the quantum side, a Graph Convolutional Network predicts QAOA variational angles (γ, β) from graph structure, replacing repeated classical optimization with a single forward pass (~1,180× latency reduction). On the biomedical side, a ClinicalGCN classifies fetal monitoring exams via physiologic similarity graphs, achieving 0.963 ROC AUC on a held-out clinical cohort. Both branches share graphs as their core representation, making the architecture modular and domain-agnostic.
