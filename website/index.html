Research Summary

This project demonstrates a unified graph-learning pipeline. On the quantum side, a GNN predicts QAOA variational angles from graph structure. On the biomedical side, graph convolutional models classify fetal monitoring exams on a physiologic similarity graph. The repository's main landing page at ../index.html is the best overview of the current results and visual assets.
